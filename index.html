My Story

From scared beginner to community builder

2020: The Beginning

I was working in retail, feeling stuck and wondering if there was more to life. A friend mentioned coding, and I laughed - \"That's not for people like me.\"

2021: First Hello World

After months of YouTube tutorials and feeling lost, I finally wrote my first program. It was just \"Hello World\" but I cried with joy. I knew I found my calling.

2022: Landing My Dream Job

With the help of an amazing mentor, I built my portfolio and landed my first developer role. The imposter syndrome was real, but so was my determination.

2023: Giving Back

I realized my journey could help others. Initiative Code was born from late-night conversations with friends who felt just as lost as I once did.
